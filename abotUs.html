About Us

Gunabai Hotel Management College is a leading institute in the field of hospitality and hotel management in the region. We are committed to providing our students with the knowledge, skills, and hands-on experience they need to succeed in this exciting industry.

Our college offers a wide range of programs and courses, including diploma and degree programs in hotel management, culinary arts, and hospitality management. We have state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members who are passionate about teaching and supporting our students.

At Gunabai Hotel Management College, we are dedicated to preparing our students for successful careers in the hospitality industry. We provide them with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their careers and become leaders in the industry.

Contact us to learn more about our programs and how you can join our community of hospitality professionals.

Director Message

As the Dean of Gunabai Hotel Management College, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our institution. Our college is committed to providing our students with the best possible education and training in the field of hospitality and hotel management.

We have a team of experienced faculty members who are dedicated to helping our students achieve their goals and become successful professionals in the industry. We also have state-of-the-art facilities and a wide range of programs and courses to meet the needs of our students.

Whether you are just starting out in your career or are looking to advance your skills and knowledge, Gunabai Hotel Management College is the place for you. We look forward to helping you achieve your goals and become a leader in the exciting and dynamic field of hospitality.

- Director Vartika Shukla

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading institute in the field of hospitality and hotel management by providing high-quality education and training to our students, and by developing innovative programs and partnerships that meet the needs of the industry.

Mission

Our mission is to provide our students with the knowledge, skills, and hands-on experience they need to succeed in the hospitality and hotel management industry. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment, and to developing partnerships with industry leaders to provide our students with valuable networking opportunities and practical training.

Objectives

  • Provide high-quality education and training to our students
  • Develop innovative programs and partnerships that meet the needs of the industry
  • Create a supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Develop partnerships with industry leaders to provide our students with valuable networking opportunities and practical training

Goals

  • Expand our course offerings to meet the evolving needs of the industry
  • Increase our student enrollment and graduation rates
  • Develop new partnerships with industry leaders and organizations
  • Improve our facilities and technology to provide the best possible learning environment for our students
