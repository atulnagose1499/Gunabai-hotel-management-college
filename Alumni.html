Our Alumni Network

At Gunabai hotel management college, we are proud of our alumni and the success they have achieved in their careers. Our alumni are leaders in the hospitality industry and continue to make an impact in their communities.

Our alumni network provides opportunities for graduates to connect with each other, share their experiences and knowledge, and expand their professional networks. Whether you are a recent graduate or have been out of school for several years, our alumni network can help you achieve your career goals.

Our alumni mission is to foster lifelong relationships between the college and its graduates, and to provide a platform for alumni to engage with the college and each other. Our goals include:

  • Providing opportunities for professional development and continuing education
  • Encouraging and promoting volunteerism and community involvement
  • Supporting the college through philanthropic activities and donations

We invite you to join our alumni network and stay connected with the Gunabai hotel management college community.

Career Services

Job Board

Our job board is regularly updated with job opportunities in the hospitality industry. Students and alumni can access the board through our online portal.

Resume Reviews

Our career advisors offer individualized feedback on resumes to help students and alumni stand out to potential employers.

Interview Prep

We provide resources and coaching to help students and alumni prepare for job interviews in the hospitality industry.

Career Fairs

We host several career fairs each year to connect students and alumni with hospitality industry employers.

Networking Events

We organize events to help students and alumni build their professional network in the hospitality industry.

Career Advising

Our career advisors provide one-on-one support to help students and alumni identify career goals and develop strategies to achieve them.
