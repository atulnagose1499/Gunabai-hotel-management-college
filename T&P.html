Our program includes 6 months of industrial training to prepare our students for real-world working environments.

Resume Building

Our faculty helps students create professional and effective resumes to showcase their skills and qualifications.

Guest Lectures

We invite industry experts to give guest lectures on various topics related to the hospitality industry.

Job Fairs

We organize job fairs to provide our students with opportunities to connect with potential employers.

Internships

We offer internship opportunities to help our students gain practical experience and develop their skills.




Application Process

Step 1

Visit the Training and Placement Office to get information about companies and available opportunities.

Step 2

Create a resume and cover letter tailored to the job you are applying for.

Step 3

Submit your application materials to the Training and Placement Office for review and feedback.

Step 4

Schedule an interview with the company if you are selected for an opportunity.

Step 5

Receive an offer and accept or decline based on your preferences.

Step 6

Notify the Training and Placement Office of your decision and keep them updated on your progress.




Placement Services

Gunabai hotel management college has a dedicated placement cell that offers a wide range of services to students to help them prepare for the job market. Some of the services offered by the placement cell include:

  • Resume writing and review
  • Mock interviews
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Job search strategies

In addition to these services, the placement cell also organizes on-campus recruitment drives, job fairs, and other events to connect students with potential employers. Many leading hotels and hospitality companies visit the campus every year to recruit students for a variety of roles.
