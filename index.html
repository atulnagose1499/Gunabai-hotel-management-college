About Us

Vision

Our vision is to be the premier hotel management college in the region, producing graduates who are sought after by top employers in the industry.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a world-class education in hotel management, combining practical skills and theoretical knowledge to prepare our students for successful careers in the industry.

Objectives
  • Provide high-quality education and training to our students
  • Facilitate research and innovation in the field of hotel management
  • Develop partnerships with leading industry organizations and employers
  • Promote the values of ethical and sustainable business practices
Goals
  • To increase the number of graduates who are employed in top-tier hotels and resorts
  • To expand our research and development programs to drive innovation in the industry
  • To establish new partnerships with industry leaders and organizations
  • To promote sustainability and ethical business practices throughout the industry


Academics Showcase

Our Team

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing the best education and support to our students. They bring a wealth of industry experience and expertise to the classroom, ensuring that our students are prepared to succeed in the world of hotel management.

Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in their fields, with years of experience in the hotel management industry. They bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the classroom, and are dedicated to helping our students succeed in their studies and careers.

Management Community

Our management community is made up of industry leaders and experts who work closely with our faculty and students to provide a wealth of resources and opportunities. They bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to our programs, ensuring that our students are prepared to succeed in the industry.

Infrastructure

Our college is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including fully-equipped classrooms, computer labs, and hands-on training facilities. Our students have access to the latest technologies and equipment, ensuring that they are prepared to succeed in the industry.

Faculty Research and Special Programs

Our faculty members are actively engaged in research and innovation in the field of hotel management, and are dedicated to bringing their findings back to the classroom. We offer a range of special programs and initiatives, including internships, mentorship programs, and research opportunities, to help our students develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the industry.
