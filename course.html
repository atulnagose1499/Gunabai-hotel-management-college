Course Catalog

Hotel Operations Management

Course Description: Hotel Operations Management is a short course that aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the operations management of the hotel industry. The course covers various topics related to hotel operations, such as front office management, housekeeping management, food and beverage management, and human resources management.

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None

Hospitality and Tourism Marketing

Course Description: Hospitality and Tourism Marketing is a short course that focuses on marketing strategies in the hospitality and tourism industry. The course covers key marketing concepts, techniques, and strategies used in the hospitality and tourism sector, including market analysis, branding, social media marketing, customer engagement, and revenue management

Duration: 3 months

Prerequisites: None

Food and Beverage Management

Course Description: Food and Beverage Management is a short course offered by Gunabai Hotel Management College. The course covers the fundamentals of food and beverage management in the hospitality industry. Students will learn how to manage food and beverage operations in various hospitality settings such as restaurants, hotels, and resorts.

Duration: 9 months

Prerequisites: Course Title 1

Hotel Revenue Management

Course Description: Hotel Revenue Management is a critical function in the hotel industry that involves maximizing revenue and profits by employing various techniques, strategies, and tools. It is a short course that provides an overview of revenue management concepts and practices in the hotel industry.

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None

Resort and Casino Management

Course Description: Resort and Casino Management is a short-term course that provides an overview of the hospitality industry with a focus on resort and casino management. This course covers topics such as the history of resorts and casinos, the role of technology in the hospitality industry, the importance of customer service, marketing and branding strategies, and event planning.

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None

Conference and Event Management

Course Description: Conference and Event Management is a short course designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to successfully plan and execute a wide range of events, such as conferences, meetings, exhibitions, product launches, and trade shows. The course covers various topics related to event management, including event design, planning, logistics, budgeting, marketing, and risk management.

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None

Hospitality Law and Ethics

Course Description: Hospitality Law and Ethics is a course that provides an overview of the legal and ethical issues relevant to the hospitality industry. The course covers topics such as contract law, negligence, liability, intellectual property, and labor and employment law, as well as ethical considerations and decision-making. Students will learn how to identify and navigate legal and ethical challenges that arise in the hospitality industry, and develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None

Housekeeping and Facilities Management

Course Description: Housekeeping and Facilities Management is a short course that provides an introduction to the fundamentals of managing a hotel's housekeeping and facility operations. The course covers topics such as planning and organizing housekeeping activities, housekeeping and maintenance standards, inventory management, and quality control. Participants will gain an understanding of the roles and responsibilities of housekeeping and facilities management personnel, as well as the importance of effective communication and teamwork in these areas.

Duration: 6 months

Prerequisites: None
