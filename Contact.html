Frequently Asked Questions

Gunabai Hotel Management College offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in hospitality and tourism management, culinary arts, and event management. Please refer to our website for more information on each program.

Admission requirements vary depending on the program, but generally include a high school diploma or equivalent, English language proficiency, and in some cases, relevant work experience. Please refer to our website for specific admission requirements for each program.

Gunabai Hotel Management College offers a range of financial aid options for eligible students, including scholarships, grants, and student loans. Please refer to our website for more information on how to apply for financial aid and the options available.
